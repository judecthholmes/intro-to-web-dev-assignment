Studio Albums

The Strokes have released six studio albums over their career, each showcasing the band's evolution while maintaining their signature sound.

Albums

Is This It

2001

The debut album that launched a revolution. "Is This It" is widely considered one of the greatest albums of the 2000s and a defining moment in the garage rock revival.

Track Listing:

  1. Is This It
  2. The Modern Age
  3. Soma

  6. Alone, Together

  9. New York City Cops
  10. Trying Your Luck
  11. Take It or Leave It

Room on Fire

2003

The sophomore album that proved The Strokes were no one-hit wonder. "Room on Fire" refined their sound while maintaining the energy of their debut.

Track Listing:

  11. I Can't Win

Angles

2011

After a five-year hiatus, The Strokes returned with "Angles", featuring a more collaborative songwriting approach and diverse musical influences.

A departure from their traditional sound, "Comedown Machine" showcased the band's willingness to experiment with synths and falsetto vocals.

The Grammy Award-winning sixth album marked a triumphant return for the band, blending their classic sound with mature songwriting and production.

Notable Singles

  • Last Nite (2001) - Their breakthrough single that defined the garage rock revival
  • Someday (2002) - A fan favorite with an iconic music video
  • Reptilia (2004) - One of their most energetic and beloved tracks
  • You Only Live Once (2006) - A powerful anthem from their third album
  • Under Cover of Darkness (2011) - The comeback single that proved they still had it
  • Bad Decisions (2020) - A nostalgic track from their Grammy-winning album
