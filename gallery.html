Album Artwork

The Strokes are known for their distinctive album artwork. "The New Abnormal" features a painting by Jean-Michel Basquiat called "Bird on Money" (1981), reflecting the band's appreciation for New York art and culture.

  • Is This It: A notably controversial cover featuring a photograph by Colin Lane
  • Room on Fire: An artwork resembling a casette machine.
  • First Impressions of Earth: A minimalistic cover - strings?
  • Angles: Colourful geometric pattern
  • Comedown Machine: A retro-style illustration
  • The New Abnormal: Jean-Michel Basquiat's "Bird on Money"
