Welcome to The Strokes Fan Site

The Strokes are an American rock band from New York City. Formed in 1998, the band is composed of singer Julian Casablancas, guitarists Nick Valensi and Albert Hammond Jr., bassist Nikolai Fraiture, and drummer Fabrizio Moretti.

Why The Strokes Matter

Garage Rock Revival

The Strokes are credited with leading the garage rock revival movement in the early 2000s, inspiring countless bands and reshaping the rock music landscape.

Critical Acclaim

Their debut album "Is This It" is widely regarded as one of the greatest albums of the 2000s and a landmark in indie rock history.

Enduring Legacy

Over two decades later, The Strokes continue to release music and tour, proving their lasting impact on rock music.

Quick Facts

  • Formed: 1998 in New York City
  • Genre: Indie Rock, Garage Rock Revival, Post-Punk Revival
  • Albums: 6 Studio Albums
  • Awards: Grammy Award for Best Rock Album (2021)
  • Record Label: RCA Records, Cult Records

Latest News

The New Abnormal Wins Grammy

In 2021, The Strokes won their first Grammy Award for Best Rock Album for "The New Abnormal", their sixth studio album released in 2020.
