About the Hall of Fame

The Rock and Roll Hall of Fame, located in Cleveland, Ohio, honors artists who have had a significant impact on the evolution, development, and perpetuation of rock and roll.

  • Founded: 1983
  • First Induction: 1986
  • Location: Cleveland, Ohio, USA
  • Eligibility: Artists become eligible 25 years after their first recording
